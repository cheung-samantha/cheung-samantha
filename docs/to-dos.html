Overview

Background

Many users mark their emails as "unread" or "starred" to remind them to complete a task related to that email, sometimes resulting in an inbox cluttered with old "unread" emails.

Product

Yahoo Mail iOS mobile and desktop

Problem Statement

By giving users an opportunity to turn an email into a concrete to-do, we not only give them the ability to easily keep track of and complete tasks associated with messages in their inbox, but we also give them the satisfaction of checking an item off of their to-do list.
